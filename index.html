Top activities in Cape Town

Visit Muizenberg Beach

The clear blue ocean alongside the vibrant huts that line the beach are a must-see when visiting Cape Town. This is also a good place to swim if that's your thing; the water is warmer here than most other beaches. Although it would be advised to read up on the rules about what flags mean "sharks". They like the warmer water too.

See the penguins!

Discover the incredibly adorable African Penguins in their natural habitat on a half-day tour to Boulders Beach. Swim or laze around on the beach while surrounded by these friendly birds.

Take a trip from Table Mountain

Hiking up to the top of Table Mountain makes the cable car ride back down that much more satisfying. If hiking isn't your thing, you can get up there by other means. Either way, the point is the stunning view you get coming down from the top.
